Support & FAQ

I bought the Full Version on another device / after reinstalling

Open the game → gear icon (Settings) → scroll to Support & Contact → Restore Purchases. The unlock is tied to your Apple ID, not the device.

Where is my progress after switching devices?

Progress syncs through your own private iCloud. Make sure both devices are signed into the same Apple ID with iCloud enabled, launch the game once on each, and the further run wins. Progress never moves backwards.

How does the free part work?

The first 8 missions of the main campaign and the first 3 missions of each Elite campaign are free, with every battle mechanic included. One purchase unlocks everything else, forever. No ads, no subscriptions.

I want a refund

Purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds go through them: reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple usually answers within a day or two.

The music or sound is acting up

Check the Music and Sound Effects switches in Settings, and the hardware mute switch on your device. If something still sounds wrong, write to us. A description plus your device model is enough.

I filed a Formal Complaint tip

It has been filed, stamped, reviewed and respectfully ignored. The tanks are training harder as we speak. Thank you. Seriously.
