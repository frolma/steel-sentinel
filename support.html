A few things people ask about

I bought the unlock but it's gone, or I got a new phone.

Open Settings in the game and tap "Restore purchases". Use the same Apple Account you bought it with and it will come back.

Where do I change difficulty or speed?

Tap the gear to open Settings. Difficulty is Recruit, Soldier or Veteran, and speed is set under Mobility Core. Both take effect on your next new game.

My progress disappeared.

Saves live on your device, so reinstalling or resetting the phone wipes them. A purchase is different: you can always get the unlock back with "Restore purchases".

There are no buttons on the battlefield.

That's on purpose. I wanted to keep the screen clean. By default (Joystick mode) the left half of the screen steers and the right half shoots. If you'd rather drag to move and tap to shoot, switch to Free Touch in Settings, under Controls.
