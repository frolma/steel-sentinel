Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

What we collect

Nothing. There is no account to create, nothing to log in to, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind. The game runs fully offline.

Your progress

Your progress, high scores and settings are kept on your device and in your own private iCloud, so they follow you to a new device. We never see them: Apple stores them under your Apple ID, and only your devices can read them.

Purchases

The optional Full Version unlock and the tip jar are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Purchases can be restored at any time from Settings → Support & Contact inside the game, and refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Children

The game contains no social features, no chat, no user-generated content and no data collection, and is rated 9+ for mild cartoon violence.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published on this page with an updated date above.
