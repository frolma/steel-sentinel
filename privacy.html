Steel Sentinel

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

I'm Maxim Frolov, and I built Steel Sentinel on my own. This page explains what the game does with your information. In short, it doesn't collect any.

What the game collects

Nothing. There is no account to create, nothing to log in to, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

Your saved games

Your progress, high scores and settings are kept on your device and nowhere else. None of it is sent to me or to anyone, and it all disappears if you delete the game.

Purchases

The game has in-app purchases, and Apple handles every part of them through the App Store. I never see your name, your card, or your billing details. If you reinstall the game or move to a new device, "Restore purchases" brings back what you already bought, using your Apple Account.

Children

The game doesn't collect data from anyone, children included. There are no ads and no chat.

If this policy changes

I'll post the new version on this page and update the date at the top.
